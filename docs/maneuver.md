# Maneuver List

This document defines **learnable maneuvers** in the world, including:

- Universal maneuvers with **no skill prerequisite**
    
- **Race‑specific maneuvers** tied to culture and physiology
    

Maneuvers are taught by **masters**, unless otherwise noted. All maneuvers still use the **unified skill check system** when executed.

---

## Universal Maneuvers (No Skill Prerequisite)

These maneuvers may be learned by **any character** who meets the **level requirement**, regardless of skills. They represent instinct, grit, or basic training.

### Brace

- **Tier:** Lower (1–5)
    
- **Cost:** Stamina
    
- **Effect:** Reduce incoming damage from the next attack
    
- **Notes:** Commonly taught by guards or mercenaries
    

---

### Push Back

- **Tier:** Lower (1–5)
    
- **Cost:** Stamina
    
- **Effect:** Attempt to force an enemy back one space
    
- **Notes:** Uses Physical for effectiveness but requires no skill
    

---

### Second Wind

- **Tier:** Lower (1–5)
    
- **Cost:** None (once per rest)
    
- **Effect:** Recover a small amount of stamina or health
    
- **Notes:** Survival instinct, no trainer required
    

---

### Focused Breath

- **Tier:** Lower (1–5)
    
- **Cost:** Time (combat round)
    
- **Effect:** Regain stamina or mana over time
    
- **Notes:** Teachable by monks, sailors, and elders
    

---

### Desperate Strike

- **Tier:** Lower (1–5)
    
- **Cost:** Stamina
    
- **Effect:** High‑risk attack with increased critical chance
    
- **Failure:** Leaves user exposed
    

---

## Race‑Specific Maneuvers

These maneuvers may only be learned by members of the listed species. Masters will refuse to teach them to outsiders.

---

## Human Maneuvers

### Adaptive Feint

- **Tier:** Lower (1–5)
    
- **Effect:** Gain a bonus on the next action after observing an enemy
    
- **Notes:** Rewards versatility and timing
    

---

## Sharah Maneuvers

### Stone Stand

- **Tier:** Lower (1–5)
    
- **Effect:** Become immovable for a short duration
    
- **Bonus:** Increased resistance to physical damage
    

---

## Gal’tar (Hobgoblin) Maneuvers

### Formation Strike

- **Tier:** Lower (1–5)
    
- **Effect:** Bonus damage when allies are adjacent
    
- **Notes:** Scales with discipline and positioning
    

---

## Dwarven Maneuvers

### Sea‑Hardened Grip

- **Tier:** Lower (1–5)
    
- **Effect:** Resist forced movement and disarms
    
- **Notes:** Learned aboard ships or coastal holds
    

---

## Toraelqui Maneuvers

### Underflow Leap

- **Tier:** Lower (1–5)
    
- **Effect:** Powerful leap that ignores terrain penalties
    
- **Bonus:** Enhanced when near water
    

---

## Jalitic (Orc) Maneuvers

### Calculated Action

- **Tier:** Lower (1–5)
    
- **Effect:** Delay action to gain accuracy or insight
    
- **Notes:** Encourages planning over brute force
    

---

## Tuskagor Maneuvers

### Boar Rush

- **Tier:** Lower (1–5)
    
- **Effect:** Charge forward, damaging and knocking back enemies
    
- **Failure:** Self‑stagger

## Skill-Gated Maneuvers

Most maneuvers beyond the earliest tier require **skill prerequisites**. These maneuvers represent trained techniques, refined magic, or specialized knowledge and can only be taught by appropriate masters.

All skill-gated maneuvers still resolve using the **unified d100 skill check system**.

---

## Universal Skill-Gated Maneuvers

### Precision Striking

- **Tier:** Mid (6–10)
    
- **Skill Requirement:** Fighting 30+
    
- **Attributes:** Physical
    
- **Effect:** Increased hit chance; ignores minor armor
    

---

### Defensive Rolling

- **Tier:** Mid (6–10)
    
- **Skill Requirement:** Dodging 30+
    
- **Attributes:** Physical, Mental
    
- **Effect:** Evade an incoming attack and reposition
    

---

### Controlled Breathing

- **Tier:** Mid (6–10)
    
- **Skill Requirement:** Meditating 25+
    
- **Attributes:** Spiritual
    
- **Effect:** Rapid stamina and mana recovery
    

---

### Disarming Strike

- **Tier:** Mid (6–10)
    
- **Skill Requirement:** Fighting 35+
    
- **Attributes:** Physical, Mental
    
- **Effect:** Attempt to disarm target
    

---

## Race-Specific Skill-Gated Maneuvers

### Human — Tactical Adaptation

- **Tier:** Mid (6–10)
    
- **Skill Requirement:** Investigating 25+ OR Fighting 25+
    
- **Effect:** Gain temporary bonus based on enemy behavior
    

---

### Sharah — Stonebound Wrath

- **Tier:** Mid (6–10)
    
- **Skill Requirement:** Channeling 30+
    
- **Attributes:** Physical, Spiritual
    
- **Effect:** Area shockwave; bonus Earth damage
    

---

### Gal’tar — Iron Discipline

- **Tier:** Mid (6–10)
    
- **Skill Requirement:** Leading 30+
    
- **Attributes:** Physical, Social
    
- **Effect:** Allies gain accuracy and morale
    

---

### Dwarves — Tidebreaker Swing

- **Tier:** Mid (6–10)
    
- **Skill Requirement:** Fighting 30+ AND Swimming 20+
    
- **Attributes:** Physical
    
- **Effect:** Heavy attack that ignores forced movement
    

---

### Toraelqui — Current Riding

- **Tier:** Mid (6–10)
    
- **Skill Requirement:** Swimming 30+
    
- **Attributes:** Physical, Spiritual
    
- **Effect:** High-speed movement through water or rain
    

---

### Jalitic — Calculated Rebuttal

- **Tier:** Mid (6–10)
    
- **Skill Requirement:** Persuading 30+ OR Deceiving 30+
    
- **Attributes:** Mental, Social
    
- **Effect:** Apply confusion or hesitation to target
    

---

### Tuskagor — Gore and Trample

- **Tier:** Mid (6–10)
    
- **Skill Requirement:** Fighting 35+
    
- **Attributes:** Physical
    
- **Effect:** Massive charge with knockdown
    

---

## Design Notes

- Skill prerequisites prevent early power spikes
    
- Mid-tier maneuvers reward focused training
    
- Race-specific maneuvers reinforce identity without locking builds
    
- High- and Epic-tier maneuvers should introduce rule-bending effects
